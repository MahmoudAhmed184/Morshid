Morshid is an AI teaching assistant for university students. It helps students understand lessons by asking guiding questions, giving hints, explaining mistakes, and using trusted course materials with clear citations. Unlike a normal chatbot, Morshid does not simply provide final answers to assignments or exams. It encourages students to think, try, and learn independently. Students can also submit code and receive debugging guidance without getting the complete solution. Instructors can upload course materials, review uncertain or reported answers, and correct the guidance. Our goal is to support honest learning, reduce repeated questions, and make AI assistance safer and more reliable.
