## Modifications Made

These PDFs are adaptations of the upstream work, not verbatim copies:

- The upstream PDF/print export of the book (retrieved 2026-07-09) was split
  into five grouped part PDFs using `pypdf`. The split was performed on page
  boundaries, so some parts carry over the tail of the preceding chapter.
- Chapter ranges: Part 1 = chapters 3–5; Part 2 = chapters 6–8;
  Part 3 = chapters 10–12; Part 4 = chapters 13–15; Part 5 = chapters 16–17.
- Chapter 9 ("Advanced Functions Example") and the upstream end-matter chapters
  (error handling, recursion, object-oriented programming, and the standard-
  library tour) were omitted from this P0 selection.

No content was otherwise rewritten; the modifications are limited to selection,
splitting, and omission as described above.
